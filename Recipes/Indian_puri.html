Indian Puri

THIS IS SO DELICIOUS

Description

Introducing to you one of the Indian dish, its eye catching, delicious with a nice cent.
the dish is perfect and easy to prepare in just few minutes - nice for break fast


Ingridients;

  • indian wheat flour
  • salt
  • water
  • ghee
  • semolina flour
  • cooking oil
  • ajwain
  • yogurt

Steps:

  1. Take 1cup of wheat flour and add 1/2 tablespoon of salt
  2. One tablespoon of semolina flour
  3. One tablespoon ghee
  4. Add a pinch of ajwain just for flavour
  5. Two tablespoon yogurt and water little by little to make tough dough
  6. Add 3cups of cooking oil, wait until hot and dip your disc like shaped puri
