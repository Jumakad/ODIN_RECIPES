Crispy Chicken


Description

The above illustrated recipe has got an amazing crispy like texture
with a golden brown colour that makes it look delicious and more attractive.
With this, really any one would wish to taste.

The recipe can possibly be served with dishes eg French Fries etc.
The dish can also be ganished with Corriander Leaves,Fried Onions.

Ingridients;

  • onions
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • corriander
  • cinamon
  • salt
  • breadcramps
  • wheat flour
  • eggs and
  • paprika
  • water
  • chicken & finally
  • cooking oil

Steps:

  1. Firstly ensure general cleanliness
  2. Dip the chicken into water mixed with garlic, onion, salt, cinamon and corriander.
  3. Leave the chicken to boil for aroud 15-20 minutes at 100 degrees celitious.
  4. During boiling session, prepare the eggs in a bowel,have your breadcramps in also a seperate bowel,
    then mix paprika and salt in wheat flour also in a seperate bowel.
  5. After drain the chicken from the spiced water
  6. Dip the chicken in eggs then into the mixture of wheat flour
  7. Transfer the chicken back to bowel of eggs then to the bowel of breadcramps
    repeat the procedure to all pieces.
