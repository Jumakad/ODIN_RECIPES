Beef Steak

Description

Enjoy the grilled beef steak spiced with salt and pepper that's ready in just 20 minutes
perfect for a dinner.

Ingredients

  • 3 beef steak ,about 3/4 inch.
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Steps

  1. prepare the charcoal or gas grill for direct heat,
    heat for about 40 minutes for charcoal or about 10 minutes for gas grill
  2. Remove the fat around the beef edges ,do not cut the beef beneath this may cook out the juice and the beef will dry
  3. Place the beef on the grill rack over medium heat and heat for 6 to 12 minutes
